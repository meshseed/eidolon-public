With the v3.5 update, Eidolon Mesh is now a fully capable Progressive Web App (PWA) optimized for mobile devices. This guide covers the new mobile-specific features and how to use the Mesh on the go.

## üöÄ Installation (PWA)

Eidolon Mesh is designed to be installed as an app on your home screen. This removes browser chrome and gives you a full-screen, immersive experience.

### iOS (Safari)
1. Open [eidolon-mesh.net](https://eidolon-mesh.net) in Safari.
2. Tap the **Share** button (box with arrow).
3. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**.
4. Launch the app from your home screen.

### Android (Chrome)
1. Open [eidolon-mesh.net](https://eidolon-mesh.net) in Chrome.
2. Tap the menu (three dots).
3. Tap **"Install App"** or **"Add to Home Screen"**.

## ‚ö° Performance Tips

- **Neuron Limit:** On older phones, lower the "Max Neurons" slider in Graph Settings (try 300-500) for smoother 3D performance.
- **Battery:** The 3D graph can use significant battery. Switch back to data/chat views when not actively exploring the graph.
- **Data Persistence:** Remember that your data lives in the browser. Using "Add to Home Screen" creates a *separate* storage container from the browser tab on iOS. **Don't delete the app icon unless you have backed up your data!**

## üîí Security on Mobile

- **Local Storage:** Your API keys and data remain on your device.
- **Public Wi-Fi:** We use strict **HTTPS**, so your connection is safe even on coffee shop Wi-Fi.
- **Privacy Mode:** Toggle the `#public` filter in the Network tab to control what you share when syncing.
